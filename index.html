About Me

I am a J.L. Doob postdoc in the Math Department at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. I am a homotopy theorist with particular interests in chromatic homotopy theory and the spectrum of topological modular forms. I am also interested in the interaction between chromatic, motivic, and equivariant homotopy theory.
