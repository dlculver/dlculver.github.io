About Me

I am a postdoc at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn, Germany. I am a homotopy theorist with particular interests in chromatic homotopy theory and the spectrum of topological modular forms. I also have growing interests in motivic and equivariant homotopy theory.
