Learning Seminar: p-adic modular forms

Spring 2020


Time and Place

Mondays 12-1:30p in 1 Illini Hall

Seminar info

Our goal for this semester is to learn about the theory of p-adic modular forms. We are mostly going to focus on Katz's moduli-theoretic interpretation of p-adic modular forms, though we will review the classical theory and Serre's approach to p-adic modular forms.

References

Below you can find some of the main references we will use in this seminar.
  • Katz's p-adic properties of p-adic moduli schemes and modular forms.
  • Gouvea's "Arithmetic of p-adic modular forms." This is available through the university's library system.
  • Serre's Formes modulaires et fonctions zeta p-adique.
  • Katz's paper on p-adic L-functions arising from moduli of elliptic curves.
